ABOUT HACKSPHERE

Join the most exciting hackathon of the year and push your limits

HackSphere 2025 is a global 48-hour online hackathon that brings together developers, designers, and creators from around the world to collaborate, innovate, and build amazing projects. Whether you're a seasoned hacker or a first-time participant, HackSphere offers an inclusive environment to learn, create, and showcase your skills.

Our mission is to foster creativity, encourage problem-solving, and provide a platform for tech enthusiasts to turn their ideas into reality. With workshops, mentorship, and exciting prizes, HackSphere 2025 promises to be an unforgettable experience for all participants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about HackSphere 2025

Who can participate in HackSphere 2025?

HackSphere is open to anyone aged 18 and above who is passionate about technology and innovation. Whether you're a student, professional, or hobbyist, we welcome you to join!

Do I need to have a team to participate?

Not at all! You can register as an individual and we'll help you find a team during our team formation session. Teams can have 1-5 members.

What if I'm a beginner?

HackSphere is beginner-friendly! We'll have workshops and mentors available to help you throughout the event. It's a great opportunity to learn and grow your skills.

Is there a registration fee?

No, HackSphere 2025 is completely free to participate in. We believe in making technology accessible to everyone.
